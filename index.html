not being advertised or communicated to the students that are being directly affected.
This lack of awareness and challenge to discover student’s passions and talents aren’t
providing students a good foundation for the future. With an online directory, we will
connect local volunteers with mentors for passionate highschool students. Help us
rekindle the community. E mālama ‘ia nā pono o ka ‘āina e na ‘ōpio.
